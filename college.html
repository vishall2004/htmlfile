Description

Nestled at the foothills of the Western Ghats, located in a sprawling 60-acre campus in Kovaipudur,
Coimbatore, Sri Krishna College of Technology (SKCT) is a vibrant institute of higher education
established in 1985 promoted by Sri Krishna Institutions. An extraordinary freedom of opportunity to
explore, to collaborate and to challenge oneself is the hallmark of the Institute.
